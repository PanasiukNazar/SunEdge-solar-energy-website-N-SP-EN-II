Privacy Policy

Welcome to our Privacy Policy. Protecting your privacy is important to us.

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy ("Policy") outlines how we collect, protect, and use personal data ("Personal Data") that you ("User", "You", or "Your") provide through our website and in relation to our Solar Energy Consulting Services. It also explains your available options regarding the use of your personal data and how you can access and update it. This Policy does not apply to the practices of companies we do not own or control, or to individuals we do not employ or manage.

2. Collection of Personal Data

We collect and store any information you knowingly provide when filling out forms on the Website. You may choose not to provide certain information, but in such cases, you may not be able to use all the features of the Website.

3. Collection of Non-Personal Data

When you visit the Website, our servers automatically log the information your browser sends. This data may include your device's IP address, browser type and version, operating system type and version, preferred language, the website you visited before coming to our Website, pages you visit on our Website, the length of time you spend on those pages, the information you search for, access times, dates, and other statistics.

4. Use of Collected Information

All collected information may be used to improve customer service and respond to customer inquiries and emails; to operate and manage our Website and Services. The non-personal data we collect will only be used to identify potential misuse cases and to create statistical information about the use of the Website. These statistical data will not be aggregated in a way that identifies a specific user.

5. Cookies

We may use cookies to collect, store, and track information required for statistical purposes to operate our Website and Services. You have the option to accept or decline cookies. Most web browsers automatically accept cookies, but you can usually modify your browser settings to refuse cookies if you prefer.

6. Google Ads and Data Collection

Our Website uses Google Ads to display relevant advertisements. Google Ads uses cookies to display ads based on previous visits by users to our Website or other websites on the Internet. Google uses the collected data to display ads that may interest you and may share this information with other Google services. You can disable personalized Google ads by visiting the Google Ads Settings page. You can also opt-out of personalized Google ads by visiting the Opt-out page of the Google advertising network at https://optout.networkadvertising.org/.

7. Security of Your Data

We secure the data you provide on servers in a controlled and secure environment, protected from unauthorized access, use, or disclosure. We take reasonable administrative, technical, and physical precautions to prevent unauthorized access, use, alteration, or disclosure of the personal data under our control.

8. Links to Other Websites

Our Website contains links to other websites that are not owned or controlled by us. Please be aware that we are not responsible for the privacy practices of these external websites. We encourage you to be aware when you leave our Website and to read the privacy policies of any website that collects personal information.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally to reflect changes in our practices or for other operational, legal, or regulatory reasons.
